# How to Add Themes on Linux

Easily customize your Linux desktop with new themes.

1. Download Themes
Visit https://www.gnome-look.org/ to download your preferred themes.

2. Extract Theme Files
tar -xvf <filename>.tar.gz   # or .tar, .gz

3. Move Theme to System Directory
sudo mv <extracted_folder> /usr/share/themes

4. Apply Theme
Open **Tweaks Tools** and select your new theme from the Appearance section.
